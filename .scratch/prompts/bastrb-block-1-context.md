# 🎯 Block 1 — Context & Inspiration

This is the first block of your prompt. It defines WHO you are, WHAT you do, WHO you target, and WHAT visual reference the AI should draw from.

---

## Template

```
Create a complete one-page landing page in a single HTML file
(CSS in <style>, JS in <script> at the bottom of body) for:

Name: [COMPANY / PROJECT NAME]
Activity: [ONE-SENTENCE DESCRIPTION OF WHAT YOU DO]
Target: [WHO YOU'RE TALKING TO]
Goal: [WHAT THE VISITOR SHOULD DO — e.g. book a call, sign up, buy]
Main CTA link: [WHATSAPP / CALENDLY / STRIPE / TALLY URL — or # for now]

VISUAL INSPIRATION:
Take strong inspiration from the design and atmosphere of [URL OF A SITE YOU LOVE].
Analyze its layout, animations, transitions, visual rhythm,
and reproduce that level of finish and polish.

Visual tone: [CHOOSE 2–3 ADJECTIVES]
→ Examples: premium & minimal / bold & energetic / elegant & warm /
  tech & clean / organic & soft / brutalist & raw / dark & immersive

Content language: [English / French / Spanish / etc.]
Tone of voice: [Formal / Casual / Direct / Friendly]
```

---

## 💡 Tips

**For the inspiration URL**, use well-known sites that AI models know well:

| Site | Style |
|------|-------|
| `linear.app` | Dark, premium, smooth animations |
| `vercel.com` | Dark, tech, minimalist |
| `stripe.com` | Light, clean, trustworthy |
| `ramp.com` | Light, warm, professional |
| `mercury.com` | Bold, modern, financial |
| `arc.net` | Colorful, playful, editorial |
| `raycast.com` | Dark, developer-focused, sharp |

**For the visual tone**, pick 2–3 adjectives that truly describe the feeling you want:
- `premium & minimal` → Think Apple, Linear
- `bold & energetic` → Think Framer, Superhuman  
- `elegant & warm` → Think Notion, Loom
- `tech & clean` → Think Vercel, Supabase
- `organic & soft` → Think Notion, Craft
