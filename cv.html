The thesis

Experiments aren’t failures. They’re signal.

I’m the Chief Product Officer at AWeber. I work with product managers and designers to craft experiences that help small businesses and creators grow their audiences through email.

I’m passionate about two things: building products that let people turn their passions into livelihoods, and sharing what I learn along the way. The center of that Venn diagram is experimentation as a way to unearth insights of all kinds.
